I work with leaders who want real value from AI — not a demo, but adoption
that lasts. The technology is the easy part. The hard part is trust, habits,
judgment, and change, and that's where my work lives.

My proof comes from the professional-services world — research licensed by six
state CPA organizations, AI pipelines in production at a statewide association —
but the approach applies anywhere people and AI have to work together.
