I partner with leaders in professional services, finance, and beyond who have
adopted AI — and now need to know whether it's actually working, and how to make
it work better for the people who use it.

Most organizations have bought the technology. Far fewer can answer the real
questions: *Are our people actually using it? Do they trust it? Is it improving
outcomes for our clients — or just generating activity?* That's the gap I help
close, with rigorous, human-centered research instead of vendor hype.
