## The context

Membership organizations plan against demographic change they can feel but
rarely quantify. Leadership needed a forecast rigorous enough to build
strategy on — and levers they could test themselves, not a static chart.

## The method

A cohort-replacement forecast designed, built, and quality-gated by an
autonomous overnight multi-agent AI run. Two candidate models were built and
judged in an independent bake-off; the winner backtested to **sub-1% error**
on the full sample and was validated against a blind holdout. The deliverable
set includes an interactive scenario simulator leadership can use to test
recruitment, retention, and pricing levers directly.

## What it demonstrates

AI-run quantitative strategy work held to research-lab standards: model
competition instead of a single guess, backtesting instead of vibes, and a
decision tool instead of a slide. The autonomous-run pattern — a defined
contract, gated quality checks, independent judging — generalizes to any
analysis too large for a single working session.
